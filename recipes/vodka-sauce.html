Pasta Vodka Sauce

Description

Everybody loves a soul-filling bowl of pasta, but usually making this desired bowl leaves behind a trail of unclean dishes and dirty kitchen counters. That is not the case for this easy to make vodka pasta sauce. With just a little bit of planning ahead and organisation, it is possible to create this sauce from scratch in under an hour and have it satisfy your bodys craving for some soul-slapping pasta this winter.

    Ingredients

  • 1 28oz can of San Marzano Peeled Tomatoes
  • 5 tabespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Onion
  • Coarse Kosher Salt
  • Large Pinch of Oregano
  • Large Pinch of Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Whole Sprig of Rosemary
  • 120 grams heavy cream
  • 50 g Vodka
  • 50 g grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 LB rigatoni(preferably bronze cut)
  • 1/4 cup reserved pasta water
  • Grated parmesan
  • Minced Parsley (for topping)

    Recipe

  1. Crush the whole peeled tomatoes with a food mill or blender (optional)
  2. Add the tomatoes, butter, and onion to a large skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Stirring occasionally, let the sauce simmer until it is reduced and thickened, about 30 minutes.
  4. The sauce can be stored in the fridge for about a week. It freezes well too.
  5. Add Marcella's tomato sauce to a large skillet over medium low heat.
  6. Bring to a simmer and add the oregano, pepper flakes, whole sprig of rosemary, heavy cream, and vodka. Stir to combine.
  7. Let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes. Discard the rosemary sprig and turn off the heat. Stir in the parmesan.
  8. OPTIONAL Blend the sauce until completely smooth with an immersion or stand blender.
  9. Taste the sauce! Season with salt as needed. Serve with pasta. Sauce will store in the fridge for several days.
